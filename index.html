Our Mission

HOSKDOG runs off the leash and stays true to the core ethos of decentralization. Our mission is to show that a meme token can be both fun and purposeful—bridging the gap between digital culture and real‑world environmental restoration. By locking portions of our supply to specific projects, we fund the rejuvenation of natural springs and habitats while celebrating the rebellious spirit that makes crypto communities thrive.

We reject gatekeepers, empty promises and rigid roadmaps. HOSKDOG is a call to action for builders and dreamers who believe in open participation and transparent stewardship. With every token distribution and every vote, we strive to prove that community‑driven initiatives can create measurable impact—on chain and off. This is a movement powered by humour, independence and a commitment to run wild together.

Our Vision

Our vision is a decentralized future where creativity, transparency and environmental stewardship are not contradictory but complementary. We imagine a world where every community member can contribute to positive change without sacrificing the freedom that blockchain provides. HOSKDOG’s vision is to cultivate a vibrant ecosystem that values authenticity over hype and action over speculation.

We see HOSKDOG evolving into a platform for collaboration—connecting artists, developers, environmentalists and token holders to collectively fund and manage restoration projects. By giving our community a voice in how resources are allocated and embracing a no‑leash, no‑limits mindset, we aim to leave a lasting legacy that transcends memes and proves the power of decentralized coordination. The journey is wild, and that’s exactly how we like it.

Tokenomics & Community Poll

The HOSKDOG supply is distributed to empower environmental restoration and community involvement. Ten per cent of the supply is locked for the Moss Bluff Restoration Project, another ten per cent for Black Sink, and ten per cent for Orange Springs. A further ten per cent is reserved for the founder, with a small portion released immediately and the remainder vested annually. Ten per cent goes to the treasury to support faucets, liquidity pools and ongoing development, while ten per cent each is airdropped to Hosky holders and Snek holders.

The remaining thirty per cent of the supply is governed by our community via an on‑chain poll. Holders can propose and vote on environmental projects or initiatives that align with our mission. When a project fails to secure regulatory approval or is completed under budget, unspent tokens return to the treasury for another round of community voting. Ready to make your voice heard?
