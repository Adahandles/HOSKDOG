Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains how HOSKDOG ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services. We are committed to transparency and compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Addresses: When you connect your Cardano wallet, we receive your public wallet address
  • Transaction Data: Blockchain transaction hashes and amounts associated with your interactions
  • Voluntary Information: Any information you provide through contact forms, feedback, or community channels

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Addresses: For rate limiting, fraud prevention, and analytics
  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and device information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns
  • Cookies: See Section 6 for detailed cookie information
  • Error Logs: Technical error information to improve service reliability

1.3 Blockchain Data

  • All blockchain transactions are public and permanently recorded on the Cardano blockchain
  • Anyone can view transaction history associated with your wallet address
  • We do not control or have the ability to delete blockchain data

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used Legal Basis

Rate Limiting IP address, wallet address Legitimate interest
Fraud Prevention IP address, usage patterns Legitimate interest
Analytics Usage data, aggregated statistics Legitimate interest
Service Improvement Error logs, feedback Legitimate interest
Legal Compliance All data as required Legal obligation

3. Data Storage and Retention

3.1 Where We Store Data

  • Server Logs: Stored on our hosting infrastructure (location dependent on hosting provider)
  • Browser Storage: Cookies and localStorage stored on your device
  • Blockchain: Transaction data permanently stored on the Cardano blockchain

3.2 How Long We Keep Data

  • Server Logs: 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • IP Addresses: 30 days for rate limiting, then anonymized
  • Error Logs: 1 year for debugging purposes
  • Cookies: See Section 6 for cookie-specific retention
  • Blockchain Data: Permanent and immutable

4. Your Privacy Rights

4.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to limitations)
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

4.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of exercising your rights

4.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our GitHub repository or community channels. We will respond within 30 days.

4.4 Limitations

We cannot delete or modify data that is permanently recorded on the blockchain, as we do not control the blockchain infrastructure.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for all website traffic
  • Access Controls: Restricted access to servers and databases
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and software updates
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary data

5.2 Your Responsibility

  • Keep your wallet private keys secure - we never ask for them
  • Use strong passwords and two-factor authentication where available
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

5.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law, typically within 72 hours of discovery.

6.2 Cookie Consent

7.2 Hosting Provider

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. When we transfer data internationally:

  • We ensure adequate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws
  • Blockchain data is inherently global and distributed

9. Children's Privacy

COPPA Compliance: Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

10. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers support "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, as there is no industry standard for how to interpret them. If a standard is established, we will update our policy accordingly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Material changes will be communicated through a prominent notice on the website. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact us through:

  • GitHub: Create an issue on our repository
  • Community Channels: Reach out through official HOSKDOG community channels

13. Data Protection Officer

As a decentralized community project, we do not have a formal Data Protection Officer. Privacy inquiries should be directed through the contact methods above.
